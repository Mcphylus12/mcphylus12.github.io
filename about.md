# About Me

My name's Kyle and you've managed to stumble across my attempt at having something slightly more that a LinkedIn page. I am a Computer Science graduate from the University of East Anglia after taking my love of problem solving to computers when I was in high school.

I have spent most of my educational time writing Java using Swing and awt to build forms and simple 2d games. I got into web with a summer internship at university writing mobile applications using Ionic and AngularJS.

My University courses included writing 3D applications using OpenGL, Embedded systems with C and Using Design Patterns to build a reusable mobile framework for Android.

As A Programmer I am an advocate of clean object oriented programming using SOLID design principles and TDD. For me the best experience is having a colleague send you a message just saying thanks for writing that one class in a way that made his life easier.

My professional experience in agile consists of using scrum and kanban. We have used both systems to manage large backlogs populated by an external customer.

On a more personal note I enjoy Team sports, Cooking, Video games and Chilling out. I am a english and grew up in Ipswich in suffolk.
